Mission Statement

The goal of the Women in Engineering's AUV team is to create an environment in which students can apply their knowledge from the classroom and bring it to the table in a unified team among other types of engineering majors. Creating an AUV brings many challenges that students will be able to learn creative problem solving methods to apply in their future in engineering.
