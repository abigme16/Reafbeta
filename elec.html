Electrical Team

Electrical Team Overview

The electronic systems for the AUV consist primarily of purchased parts with the exception of the power distribution Printed Circuit Board (PCB). A VectorNav VN-100 Attitude and Heading Reference System (AHRS) will collect information about the vehicleâ€™s orientation, while two MEAS US300 pressure sensors will be used for depth measurement and for leak detection. Two cameras, one facing forward and one facing down, will be used to capture video for image processing. A temperature sensor will also be placed in the hull to ensure that all electronics are operating at a safe temperature. Two 14.8 V 10000mAh Lithium Polymer batteries will be placed in parallel and serve as the power supply to the M4 ATX power supply from Minibox to achieve a maximum available current of 20 A. All of this information is relayed to the processing unit which will determine the best course of action to make.
